Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers about our catering services, menu options, and booking process.

Do you cater corporate events in Prosper?

Yes, we provide catering for corporate events of all sizes in Prosper and the surrounding DFW area. Contact us to discuss your event needs.

Are vegetarian and non-veg options available?

We offer a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Our menu can be customized to suit your preferences and dietary requirements.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially for larger events or during peak seasons.

Do you provide food booths for outdoor events?

Yes, we can set up food booths for outdoor gatherings, festivals, and community events. Let us know your event details for a tailored setup.
